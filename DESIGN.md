# Trendwire - Design System

## Brand DNA
- **Archetype**: The Sage & The Creator.
- **Vibe**: Authoritative, engineered, high-frequency trading terminal meets invite-only engaging content.
- **Voice**: "Initialize Growth Engine", "Unlock Pipeline". No fluff.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#0b1626` (Deep Navy/Black)
- **Surface**: `#1a2639` (Lighter Navy/Glass)
- **Border**: `#2d3b55` (Subtle Grid)
- **Accent (Primary)**: `#f05a1a` (Electric Orange/Red - "Growth")
- **Text (Primary)**: `#f8fafc` (White/Slate-50)
- **Text (Secondary)**: `#94a3b8` (Slate-400)

## Typography
- **Headers / Data**: `JetBrains Mono` or `Fira Code` (Monospace).
- **Body**: `Inter` or `Geist Sans`.

## UI Patterns
- **Glassmorphism**: Backdrop blur with thin, translucent borders.
- **Bento Grid**: Rigid, masonry-like layout for content cards.
- **Micro-interactions**: Hover glows, smooth transitions (0.2s ease-out).
- **Icons**: Lucide (Thin stroke: 1.5px).

## Components

### Card (Intel Brief)
- 1px border (`border-slate-800`).
- Hover: Border color shift to Accent (`#f05a1a`).
- Content: Tag (Mono), Date, Headline (Bold), Impact Score (Badge).

### Button (Action)
- Solid Accent background or Outline with glow.
- Uppercase, Monospace font.
- Sharp corners or slight radius (sm).
